What Do You Want Your Major To Be in College?

How many times have you been asked this question and had no idea what to say? Whether you haven't even thought about it or have already planned out your career path, we're here to help you feel secure about your decision.

What To Consider
While choosing what you'd like your major to be in college, there are some things to consider:

  • What are you interested/passionate about? Is this something you'd enjoy doing for 35+ years?
  • How much can you earn while pursuing this career?
  • Which schools offer this major? Are these schools at he top of my list?
  • What is the work load like? How much time will I have to sacrifice?
  • What is the feedback of those who have already completed this major?
